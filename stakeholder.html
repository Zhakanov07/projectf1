Grand Prix Motors — Power/Interest Matrix & Stakeholder Management

Part 1. Project Description

Grand Prix Motors is an IT project aimed at developing a comprehensive web platform for an exclusive Formula 1 car dealership. The platform enables customers to browse, explore through interactive 3D viewers, and purchase authentic championship-winning F1 cars spanning from 1970 to 2025. The system integrates a product catalog with advanced filtering, a secure high-value transaction payment gateway, worldwide logistics tracking, and FIA-certified car authentication workflows. Key technical components include a responsive front-end with WebGL-powered 3D car visualization, a CMS for inventory management, and third-party API integrations for payment processing and shipping. The project follows Agile methodology with 2-week sprints, targeting a launch before the Formula 1 season opener to maximize marketing impact. The total budget is estimated at $2.5M with a 12-month delivery timeline, involving a cross-functional team of developers, designers, F1 domain experts, and business analysts.
