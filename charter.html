Our History

More than half a century in the world of Formula 1.

Grand Prix Motors was founded in 1971 by a group of motorsport enthusiasts in Monaco. Starting from a small garage, we became the world's largest Formula 1 car dealership.

Over 55 years we have sold more than 200 cars to collectors and museums worldwide. Our clients include royal families, tech company founders, and motorsport legends.

Today Grand Prix Motors is a team of 40 specialists, including former engineers from Ferrari, Mercedes and McLaren, who ensure the authenticity, maintenance and restoration of every car.

1971
