Behavioural Design Pattern

Iterator Pattern

Provide a way to access the elements of an aggregate object sequentially without exposing its underlying representation.

01

What is the Iterator Pattern?

The Iterator Pattern lets you traverse a collection without exposing its internals. Here we walk through a pit stop sequence step-by-step.

UML Structure

Context -> Collection -> Iterator
- PitStopCollection
- PitStopIterator

Analogy

Imagine a pit stop checklist. The iterator lets you move to the next task, go back, jump to the first or last, and inspect progress.

JavaScript Example

Use the `PitStopIterator` to navigate tasks without exposing the underlying array directly.
